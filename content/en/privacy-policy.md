### Tracking Tech and Cookies
We use Cookies and similar tracking tech to keep tabs on activity on our Service and store certain bits of info. The tracking technologies we use include beacons, tags, and scripts. These are for collecting and tracking info, plus improving and analyzing our Service. The tech we might use includes:

- **Cookies or Browser Cookies**: A Cookie is a tiny file dropped onto your device. You can tell your browser to say 'no thanks' to all Cookies or to give you a heads-up when one is being sent. But hey, if you don't accept Cookies, you might not be able to use some parts of our Service. Our Service might use Cookies unless you've tweaked your browser settings to reject them.
- **Web Beacons**: Certain parts of our Service and our emails might contain small electronic files called Web Beacons (also known as clear gifs, pixel tags, and single-pixel gifs). These files let the Company count users who've visited those pages or opened an email, and for other related website stats (like checking out a section's popularity and verifying system and server integrity).

Cookies can be 'Persistent' or 'Session' Cookies. Persistent Cookies chill on your personal computer or mobile device when you're offline, while Session Cookies get deleted the moment you close your web browser.

We use both Session and Persistent Cookies for the purposes outlined below:

-   **Necessary / Essential Cookies**
    -   **Type**: Session Cookies
    -   **Managed by**: Us
    -   **Purpose**: These Cookies are super important for giving you services available through the Website and letting you use some of its features. They help verify users and prevent dodgy use of user accounts. Without these Cookies, the services you've asked for can't be provided, and we only use these Cookies to give you those services.

-   **Cookies Policy / Notice Acceptance Cookies**
    -   **Type**: Persistent Cookies
    -   **Managed by**: Us
    -   **Purpose**: These Cookies are used to figure out if users have accepted the use of cookies on the Website.

-   **Functionality Cookies**
    -   **Type**: Persistent Cookies
    -   **Managed by**: Us
    -   **Purpose**: These Cookies let us remember choices you make when you use the Website, like recalling your login deets or language preferences. The whole point of these Cookies is to give you a more personalized experience, so you don't have to re-enter your preferences every time you hit up the Website.

For more info about the Cookies we use and your choices regarding Cookies, hit up our Cookies Policy or the Cookies section of our Privacy Policy.
