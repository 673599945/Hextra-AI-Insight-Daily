Cookies can be either 'Persistent' or 'Session' Cookies. Persistent Cookies chill on your personal computer or mobile device even when you go offline, while Session Cookies vanish the moment you close your web browser.

We rock both Session and Persistent Cookies for these reasons:

- **Necessary / Essential Cookies**
  - **Type**: Session Cookies
  - **Managed by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are super important for giving you the services available through the Website and letting you use some of its cool features. - **Cookies Policy / Notice Acceptance Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Managed by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are all about remembering if users have said 'yes' to the use of Cookies on the Website.

- **Functionality Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Managed by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies let us remember the choices you make when you're using the Website, like keeping your login deets or language prefs in mind. The whole point of these Cookies is to give you a more personalized experience and save you from typing in your preferences every single time you hit up the Website.
