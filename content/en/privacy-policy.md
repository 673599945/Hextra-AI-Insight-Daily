### Tracking Tech & Cookies: What's the Deal?
We use Cookies and similar tracking technologies to keep an eye on what's happening on our Service and to stash away some info. The tracking tech we're talking about includes things like beacons, tags, and scripts. Their job? To collect and track info, then help us jazz up and analyze our Service. So, what kind of tech might we be using? Check it out:

- **Cookies or Browser Cookies**: A Cookie is just a tiny file popped onto your Device. You can totally tell your browser to reject all Cookies or give you a heads-up when one's being sent. But, heads up: if you don't play ball with Cookies, you might miss out on some parts of our Service. Unless you've tweaked your browser settings to say "no thanks" to Cookies, our Service will probably use 'em.
- **Web Beacons**: Some sections of our Service and our emails might have these tiny electronic files called Web Beacons (you might also know them as clear gifs, pixel tags, or single-pixel gifs). What do they do? They let the Company count users who've visited those pages or opened an email, and they help with other cool website stats (like seeing how popular a certain section is or making sure our systems and servers are all good).

Cookies? They can be either "Persistent" or "Session" Cookies. Persistent Cookies are the ones that hang around on your personal computer or mobile device even after you've gone offline. Session Cookies, though? They vanish into thin air the second you close your web browser. Poof! üí®

So, why do we use both Session and Persistent Cookies? We've got a few reasons, check 'em out:

- **Necessity/Essential Cookies**
  - **Type**: Session Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies? They're absolutely critical for hooking you up with the services available through the Website and letting you use its awesome features. They're also super helpful for authenticating users and keeping those pesky fraudulent accounts at bay. Without these Cookies, the services you're asking for just wouldn't work, and we *only* use them to provide you with exactly what you need.

- **Cookies Policy / Notification Acceptance Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are basically our way of remembering if you've given us the green light to use Cookies on the Website. Simple as that! ‚úÖ

- **Functionality Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are the brainy ones that let us remember the choices you make when you're rocking the Website. Think remembering your login deets or your preferred language. The whole point? To give you a more personalized experience, so you don't have to keep re-typing your preferences every single time you visit. Sweet!

For more info on the Cookies we use and your options concerning them, you can always cruise over to our Cookies Policy or hit up the Cookies section of our Privacy Policy. Knowledge is power, right? üí°
